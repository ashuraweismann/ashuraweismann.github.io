## 🚀 Understanding Internet Speed: What Really Matters?

When people talk about “fast internet,” most think it’s just about speed...

### 📡 Bandwidth
Bandwidth is the maximum data your connection can handle.

### ⚡ Mbps
Measured in Megabits per second.

### ⬇️ Download
Speed of receiving data.

### ⬆️ Upload
Speed of sending data.

### ⏱️ Latency
Delay in communication.

### 📊 Throughput
Actual speed you experience.

### 🔁 Jitter
Connection stability.
